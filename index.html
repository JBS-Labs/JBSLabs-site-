Why We Build

JBS Labs is an independent app development studio focused on building clear, useful tools that help people think better, navigate better, and live with more intention. We design and build apps that solve real problems without noise, gimmicks, or unnecessary complexity. Some of our projects explore mental and emotional wellbeing, others focus on navigation, coordination, and real-world utility. Each product is developed with care, restraint, and long-term usefulness in mind.

PROJECTS

MIND MENDR

A guided space for reflection, clarity, and emotional self-repair. MIND MENDR is a private, self-guided reflection app designed to help users slow down, examine their thoughts, and regain perspective during difficult moments. Rather than offering generic advice or surface-level motivation, MIND MENDR uses structured questions, intelligent analysis, and gentle guidance to help users: Understand what they're actually feeling Identify patterns, distortions, and unhelpful narratives Reframe experiences with clarity and self-compassion Move forward with greater emotional awareness MIND MENDR is not a replacement for therapy — it's a practical tool for moments when someone needs space to think, reflect, and recalibrate. Built for privacy, simplicity, and depth.

COMING NEXT

TEAM TRACKR

A real-time navigation and coordination tool. JBS Labs' next project, TEAM TRACKR, shifts focus from internal clarity to external coordination. TEAM TRACKR is a navigation and tracking app designed to help teams, crews, and groups locate and coordinate with each other in real time — whether on job sites, events, outdoor activities, or shared operations. This project reflects JBS Labs' broader mission: building tools that reduce friction, improve awareness, and support people in real-world situations.
