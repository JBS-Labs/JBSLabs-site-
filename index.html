PROJECTS

MindMendr

A guided space for reflection, clarity, and emotional self-repair. MindMendr is a private, self-guided reflection app designed to help users slow down, examine their thoughts, and regain perspective during difficult moments. MindMendr includes test and coaching functions that support deeper self-understanding:

  • Personality test
  • Attachment style test
  • Love language test
  • Goals coach

Rather than offering generic advice or surface-level motivation, MindMendr uses structured questions, intelligent analysis, and gentle guidance to help users:

  • Understand what they're actually feeling
  • Identify patterns, distortions, and unhelpful narratives
  • Reframe experiences with clarity and self-compassion
  • Move forward with greater emotional awareness

MindMendr is not a replacement for therapy - it's a practical tool for moments when someone needs space to think, reflect, and recalibrate.

Built for privacy, simplicity, and depth.

MindMendr Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

  1. Information We Handle
    MindMendr may handle the information you enter in the app, including display name, therapy chat text, goals text, reflective answers, notes, and test responses. Subscription status is also handled to unlock premium features.
  2. How Information Is Used
    Information is used for app functionality: generating AI responses, showing your saved progress, restoring purchases, and supporting reminders you choose to enable.
  3. Third-Party Services
    MindMendr uses third-party providers for core features, including AI response generation and subscription management. Data needed for these features may be transmitted through those services.
  4. Purchases
    Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple App Store (or Google Play on Android) and RevenueCat. MindMendr does not store your full payment card details.
  5. Notifications
    If you enable notifications, MindMendr may schedule reminder notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in device settings.
  6. Tracking and Advertising
    MindMendr does not use your personal data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.
  7. Your Choices
    You can manage app data on your device, disable reminders, and manage subscriptions through App Store settings. Contact JBS Labs for privacy-related questions or requests.
  8. Policy Updates
    This policy may be updated over time. Any updates will appear in the app and/or on the official MindMendr website.
